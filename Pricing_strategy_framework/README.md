# Pricing Strategy Framework

## Objective
To develop a data-driven pricing strategy framework for a company XYZ, aimed at improving pricing decisions and revenue outcomes.

## Business Context
This project was completed as part of an analytics assessment for XYZ company. The objective was to evaluate pricing-related data and design a structured framework that can support pricing decisions across products or segments.


## Dataset
- Assessment-provided / simulated pricing and sales data
- Data adapted for academic and portfolio demonstration purposes

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab

## Approach
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Analysis of price, demand, and revenue relationships
- Identification of pricing drivers
- Development of a pricing strategy framework

## Key Insights
- Identified demand sensitivity across different price ranges
- Highlighted pricing bands with higher revenue potential
- Observed impact of price changes on volume and revenue

## Pricing Strategy Framework
The proposed framework includes:
- Data-driven price benchmarking
- Demand elasticity assessment
- Revenue optimization evaluation
- Periodic pricing review recommendations

## Outcome
This project demonstrates analytical thinking, business problem-solving, and the ability to translate data insights into practical pricing strategy recommendations.

This project was completed as part of a company assessment during my MBA in Data Science. All company-identifying information has been removed to ensure confidentiality.
